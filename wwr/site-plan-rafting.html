Rafting Site Plan

Hyrum Wines

Overview

Purpose

To have a website where people can reserve rafts, times to go rafting, and even a rafting guide for beginners

Audience

  • Who is the target audience of the site?: The audience for this site will primarily be tourists looking for an adventure while on vacation or young adults seeking for a local adventure that is differnet from their normal life.
  • What will vistitors find when visiting the website?: Visitors will find a site to reserve rafting times & guides for rafting. They'll also be able to find some info on why to go rafting and basic pricing information
  • How will this site be primarily accessed?: Since this site will be used and sought for during the planning and preparing protions of an adventure to go rafting, I expect that this site will be primarily accessed via either a desktop/laptop.
  • On what device will this website be found on primarily?: Do to the prevelent nature of using phones to do basic research on nearly everything I expect the site to be found on the phone first & then transistion to the laptop/desktop.
